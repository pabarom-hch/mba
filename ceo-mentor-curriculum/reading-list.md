# Complete Reading List

> "In my whole life, I have known no wise people who didn't read all the time — none, zero." — Charlie Munger

## How to Use This List

This reading list supports the 52-week curriculum. Books are organized by mentor and by quarter. You don't need to read everything—focus on the **Essential** works first, then expand based on interest and time.

**Recommended pace:** 1 book per month minimum, ideally 2.

## Reading Protocols

### How to Read for Wisdom (Not Just Information)

1. **Read with a pen** — Mark passages that strike you
2. **Pause and reflect** — When something resonates, stop and think
3. **Connect to experience** — How does this apply to your life?
4. **Discuss with others** — Ideas clarify through conversation
5. **Review notes** — Return to marked passages periodically

### Reading Speed by Type

- **Philosophy/Wisdom:** Slow, reflective (20-30 pages/hour)
- **Biography:** Moderate (30-50 pages/hour)
- **Frameworks/How-to:** Active, with notes (25-40 pages/hour)

### When You Fall Behind

1. Focus on Essential books first
2. Use audio versions during commutes
3. Read summaries, but return to primary sources later
4. The curriculum continues even if reading lags
