## The Complete Burning Desire Framework

### The Assessment: Where Are You?

**The three levels:**
- Wishing: Passive hoping, no action, stops at obstacles
- Wanting: Active interest, some action, persists sometimes
- Burning: All-consuming, constant action, unstoppable

**The honest question:**
- Which level is your desire at?
- What evidence supports your answer?
- Is that level sufficient for your purpose?

### The Cost: What Does Lukewarm Cost?

**The visible costs:**
- Missed achievement
- Wasted time
- Wasted resources
- Wasted energy

**The hidden costs:**
- Self-trust erosion
- Opportunity cost
- Identity erosion
- Inspiration to others

### The Intensification: How Do You Elevate?

**The techniques:**
- Vivid visualization
- Raising stakes
- Connecting to deep "why"
- Proximity to success
- Burning bridges
- Dissatisfaction inventory

**The choice:**
- Which techniques resonate with you?
- Which will you practice?
- When will you start?

### The Commitment: Burning Bridges

**The logic:**
- Options reduce commitment
- Eliminated alternatives intensify focus
- When there's no Plan B, Plan A must work

**The caution:**
- Burn strategically, not impulsively
- Burn after validation
- Burn proportionally to confidence

### The Fuel: Dissatisfaction

**The sources:**
- Current reality that's unacceptable
- Past you won't return to
- Potential you're wasting
- Injustice you must address

**The combination:**
- Push (away from) + Pull (toward)
- Dual fuel source
- More resilient desire

### The Protection: Guarding the Flame

**The threats:**
- Negative people
- Self-doubt
- Failure and setbacks
- Comfort and complacency
- Distraction

**The strategies:**
- Environment control
- Mental discipline
- Failure reframing
- Urgency maintenance
- Focus protection

## Key Principles Summary

### From Hill

1. **Desire intensity determines outcome.** Weak desire = weak results.

2. **Burning desire is cultivated.** It can be deliberately intensified.

3. **The cost of lukewarm is high.** And it compounds over time.

4. **Burning bridges intensifies commitment.** When there's no Plan B, Plan A must work.

5. **Dissatisfaction is fuel.** What you're running from matters.

6. **Desire must be protected.** Negativity, doubt, and comfort threaten it.

### The Integration

**Assessment + Cost Recognition + Intensification + Commitment + Dissatisfaction + Protection = Burning Desire**

Each element is necessary. Together they create desire that cannot be extinguished.
