## The Complete BUILD Framework

### The External Building (Carnegie)

**Build through people:**
- Mastermind alliance
- Excellence in hiring
- Delegation with trust
- People are the strategy

**Build through systems:**
- Vertical integration
- Cost leadership
- Operational excellence
- Efficiency as advantage

**Build with purpose:**
- Wealth as tool
- Stewardship responsibility
- Legacy beyond accumulation
- Significance over success

### The Internal Building (Hill)

**Build the foundation:**
- Definite purpose (direction)
- Burning desire (fuel)
- Unshakeable faith (conviction)

**Build the execution:**
- Organized plan (path)
- Quick decision (initiation)
- Relentless persistence (continuation)

**Build the amplification:**
- Mastermind alliance (multiplication)
- Energy management (sustainability)
- Transmutation (conversion)

### The Integration

**External + Internal:**
- Carnegie's external methods need Hill's internal conditions
- Hill's internal work needs Carnegie's external structures
- Both are required
- Together, they create complete achievement capacity
