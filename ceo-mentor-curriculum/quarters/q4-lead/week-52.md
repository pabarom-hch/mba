## Key Takeaways from 52 Weeks

### From Q1 (Think)
- Build a latticework of mental models
- Invert problems to find solutions
- Stay within your circle of competence
- Recognize and counteract biases
- Think in decades, not days

### From Q2 (Build)
- Surround yourself with excellence
- Have a definite major purpose
- Plan and persist
- Build through others
- Wealth carries responsibility

### From Q3 (Be)
- Philosophy determines results
- Daily disciplines compound
- Associations shape you
- Be the change you seek
- Lead by serving

### From Q4 (Lead)
- Use adversity as development
- Reconciliation beats revenge
- Leadership is influence
- Develop leaders, not followers
- Build legacy through succession
