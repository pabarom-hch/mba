# Simulation: Blue Sheet Builder

> Strategic Selling Opportunity Analysis

---

## Overview

The Blue Sheet is the core tool of Miller Heiman's Strategic Selling methodology. In this simulation, you will build a complete Blue Sheet analysis for a real sales opportunity.

**Learning Objectives:**
- Apply the Strategic Selling framework
- Map buying influences in complex B2B sales
- Identify red flags and strengths
- Develop a win strategy

**Time Required:** 45-60 minutes

---

## Your Scenario

You are a sales manager at a professional services firm. You've identified a significant opportunity with a state government agency. The opportunity is:

**Opportunity Details:**
- Client: State Health Department
- Project: Public Health Data Modernization
- Value: $850,000 (12-month engagement)
- Stage: Discovery (30% probability)
- Competition: 3 known competitors
- Decision Timeline: 60 days

## Exercise 1: Single Sales Objective

Write your Single Sales Objective (SSO). A good SSO is:
- Specific about what you're selling
- Measurable (specific value)
- Time-bound (specific close date)
- Achievable (realistic given current state)

**Template:**
"By [DATE], gain approval from [CLIENT] to award a $[VALUE] contract for [SPECIFIC SERVICES]."

**Your SSO:**
```
By _______________, gain approval from _______________
to award a $____________ contract for ________________
__________________________________________________.
```

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Specific date included
- [ ] Specific decision-maker or body named
- [ ] Exact value stated
- [ ] Specific services described
- [ ] Realistic given current stage
