# Exit Preparation Workshop

## PE Turnaround: TechServices Corp - Preparing for Exit

**Estimated Time**: 60 minutes
**Difficulty**: Advanced
**Prerequisites**: PE Curriculum Phase 8 (Exit Strategy)

---

## Learning Objectives

By completing this simulation, you will:
1. Assess exit readiness across key dimensions
2. Build a compelling value creation bridge
3. Prepare sell-side materials and management presentations
4. Evaluate potential buyer universe and likely valuations
5. Plan the exit timeline and marketing process

---
