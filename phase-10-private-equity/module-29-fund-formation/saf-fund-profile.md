# SAF Partners Fund I — Case Study Profile

> A comprehensive fund formation case study following the launch of an emerging manager's first private equity fund, with HCH Enterprises as the anchor platform investment.

---

## Executive Summary

**SAF Partners Fund I, LP** ("SAF" — Single Acquisition Fund) is a $75M lower mid-market private equity fund focused on acquiring and transforming government IT services companies. This case study follows the fund from thesis development through first close, providing a realistic worked example for each stage of fund formation.

The fund's first investment is **HCH Enterprises**, a $6.3M revenue government IT consulting firm currently operating at a loss but with strong underlying fundamentals. The SAF team sees HCH as the anchor platform for a buy-and-build strategy in the fragmented government IT services sector.
