## Section 2: First 100 Days Plan (25 points)

You have just closed on a $40M revenue manufacturing company with the following characteristics:

**Company Profile:**
- 12% EBITDA margin (industry benchmark: 18%)
- DSO: 58 days (benchmark: 45 days)
- No price increase in 4 years
- Founder CEO retiring (staying 6 months to transition)
- No CFO (controller handles finance)
- Outdated ERP system
- Top customer: 30% of revenue

**Develop a First 100 Days Plan:**

a) List 5 key assessment activities for Days 1-30 (5 points)

b) Identify 3 quick wins to capture in 100 days with estimated impact (10 points)

c) Design the governance structure (board, reporting, KPIs) (5 points)

d) Outline the stakeholder communication plan (5 points)

## Section 3: Value Creation Bridge (25 points)

A PE firm acquires a $60M revenue distribution company with the following:

**Entry:**
- EBITDA: $6M (10% margin)
- DSO: 65 days
- DIO: 55 days
- DPO: 30 days
- Management team rated B-average

**Target exit (Year 4):**
- Revenue: $85M
- EBITDA margin: 15%
- Industry-benchmark working capital

**Questions:**

a) Calculate the EBITDA improvement needed and break it into components (Revenue growth EBITDA, Margin expansion EBITDA) (10 points)

b) Identify and quantify 3 specific operational initiatives to achieve the margin improvement (10 points)

c) Calculate the working capital cash release opportunity (5 points)

## Section 4: Case Analysis (30 points)

**ABC Services Case:**

ABC Services is a $50M revenue professional services firm acquired by a PE firm 6 months ago. The 100-day plan achieved some quick wins, but momentum has stalled. Current situation:

- Revenue growth: 3% (plan was 8%)
- EBITDA margin: 14% (plan was 16%)
- Two senior partners have resigned
- New CFO struggling to implement financial reporting
- ERP implementation delayed 6 months
- Sales pipeline down 25% from close

**Questions:**

a) Diagnose the root causes of the performance gap (10 points)

b) Recommend 3 immediate actions to stabilize the situation (10 points)

c) Propose a revised 12-month value creation plan (10 points)

**Calculation for Question 5:**
- $100M revenue / 365 days = $274K per day
- 5 days × $274K = $1.37M ≈ $1.4M

### Section 2 Rubric

**a) Assessment Activities (5 points - 1 each):**
- Financial baseline validation (true run-rate EBITDA)
- Customer concentration risk assessment (especially 30% customer)
- Management team assessment (CEO transition, CFO gap)
- Pricing analysis (4 years without increase)
- Operations review (ERP, processes)

**b) Quick Wins (10 points):**
- Price increase (5-8%): +$2-3M revenue, +$2-3M EBITDA (4 points)
- AR collection focus (reduce DSO): ~$500K cash release (3 points)
- Vendor renegotiation: +$100-200K EBITDA (3 points)

**c) Governance (5 points):**
- Monthly board meetings initially (1 point)
- Weekly CEO/PE operating calls (1 point)
- Weekly flash report, monthly full package (1 point)
- KPIs: Revenue, EBITDA, cash, DSO, customer concentration (2 points)

**d) Communication (5 points):**
- Day 1 employee town hall (1 point)
- Week 1 top customer outreach (2 points)
- Week 2-3 key supplier communication (1 point)
- Ongoing transition planning with founder (1 point)

### Section 3 Rubric

**a) EBITDA Bridge (10 points):**
- Current: $60M × 10% = $6M EBITDA
- Target: $85M × 15% = $12.75M EBITDA
- Improvement needed: $6.75M
- Revenue growth portion: ($85M - $60M) × 10% = $2.5M (assuming existing margin on growth)
- Margin expansion portion: $85M × 5% = $4.25M

**b) Operational Initiatives (10 points):**
- Pricing optimization (3-5% increase): +$1.8-2.5M (3 points)
- Procurement/strategic sourcing: +$1.0-1.5M (3 points)
- Labor/overhead efficiency: +$0.5-1.0M (3 points)
- Must be specific with quantification (1 point)

**c) Working Capital (5 points):**
- Current: DSO 65, DIO 55, DPO 30 = CCC 90 days
- Target: DSO 45, DIO 40, DPO 40 = CCC 45 days (benchmarks)
- Improvement: 45 days
- Cash release: $60M / 365 × 45 = $7.4M

### Section 4 Rubric

**a) Root Causes (10 points - 2 each):**
- Talent exodus (partner resignations creating capability/relationship loss)
- CFO implementation struggles (financial visibility gap)
- Technology delay (ERP ripple effects)
- Sales pipeline decline (commercial execution failure)
- Over-reliance on quick wins without sustainable foundation

**b) Immediate Actions (10 points):**
- Retention program for remaining key talent with equity/incentives (3 points)
- CFO assessment and support/replacement decision (3 points)
- Sales leadership intervention and pipeline recovery plan (3 points)
- Must prioritize and sequence (1 point)

**c) 12-Month Plan (10 points):**
- Realistic targets (revised down from original) (2 points)
- Specific initiatives by quarter (3 points)
- Resource allocation (hiring, investment) (2 points)
- Risk mitigation (customer retention, talent) (2 points)
- Governance and accountability (1 point)
