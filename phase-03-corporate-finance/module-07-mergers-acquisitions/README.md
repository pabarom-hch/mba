# Module 7: Mergers & Acquisitions

## Overview

This module covers the strategic, financial, and process aspects of M&A transactions. You'll learn how deals are originated, structured, negotiated, and executed—from both buyer and seller perspectives.

**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Prerequisites:** Module 6 (Valuation)

---

## Learning Objectives

By completing this module, you will be able to:

1. Understand strategic rationales for M&A transactions
2. Analyze acquisition targets and evaluate fit
3. Structure transactions and understand deal mechanics
4. Navigate the M&A process from both sides
5. Perform synergy analysis and accretion/dilution modeling
6. Understand hostile takeovers and defense mechanisms
7. Integrate acquired companies effectively

## Key Frameworks

### M&A Strategic Rationale Matrix

```
                    Horizontal         Vertical          Conglomerate
                   (Same Industry)    (Value Chain)     (Diversified)
                   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
Synergies          • Market share     • Supply security  • Limited
                   • Cost reduction   • Margin capture   • Financial
                   • Pricing power    • Quality control  • Tax benefits

Examples           • Bank mergers     • Oil & refinery   • Berkshire
                   • Airline deals    • Tech + chips     • GE (historical)
                   • Pharma           • Retail + supply
```

### Deal Process Timeline

```
Week:    1-4      5-8       9-12     13-16     17-20    21-24
         │        │         │         │         │        │
         ▼        ▼         ▼         ▼         ▼        ▼
      Strategy  Target   Initial    Due      Negotiate  Close
      & Screen  Approach Contact  Diligence  & Sign

Key Activities:
- Screen targets    - NDA          - Management    - Purchase    - Regulatory
- Build model       - Info request   meetings       agreement    - Financing
- Board approval    - Indicative   - Site visits  - Definitive  - Integration
  to pursue          bid           - Detailed DD    docs          planning
```

### Sources of Synergy Value

```
Cost Synergies (More Certain)
├── Headcount reduction
├── Facilities consolidation
├── Procurement savings
└── Technology rationalization

Revenue Synergies (Less Certain)
├── Cross-selling opportunities
├── Geographic expansion
├── Product bundling
└── Pricing power
```

## CEO Relevance

### As an Acquirer

Understanding M&A enables you to:
- Evaluate "build vs. buy" decisions strategically
- Lead due diligence with appropriate rigor
- Negotiate effectively with sellers
- Set realistic synergy expectations
- Drive successful integration

### As a Potential Target

Understanding M&A helps you:
- Recognize when your company may attract interest
- Prepare the organization for potential sale
- Maximize value through proper positioning
- Respond appropriately to unsolicited approaches
- Protect stakeholder interests

### Strategic Questions

1. **Should we pursue M&A?** - Is acquisition the right strategy vs. organic growth?
2. **What should we pay?** - How much value can we create and share?
3. **How do we integrate?** - Can we capture the synergies promised?
4. **What if we're approached?** - Are we prepared to respond?
