## Key Takeaways

1. **Portfolio frameworks provide structure** - BCG, GE-McKinsey, Parenting
2. **Each has limitations** - Use multiple perspectives
3. **Parenting advantage is key** - Best owner, not just good business
4. **Resource allocation is strategic** - Not political or historical
5. **Portfolio balance matters** - Growth, cash, risk, coherence
6. **Divestiture is strategic** - Know when to exit
7. **Regular portfolio review** - Annual strategic assessment
8. **Execution matters** - Framework alone doesn't create value
