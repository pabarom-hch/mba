# PRD: Private Equity Buy & Build Curriculum

> Comprehensive curriculum for executing PE turnaround and buy-and-build strategies

---

## Executive Summary

This curriculum provides an end-to-end education for executing a Private Equity buy-and-build strategy, from initial thesis development through exit. Designed for operators who will execute these strategies in real life, it combines academic frameworks with practical playbooks, financial models, and case studies.

**Target Outcome**: Complete knowledge to identify, acquire, transform, and exit a platform company while executing 3-5 add-on acquisitions.

## Content Requirements

### Lesson Format

Each lesson will include:
1. **Learning Objectives** (3-5 per lesson)
2. **Core Content** (markdown with headers, lists, tables)
3. **Key Concepts** (terms and definitions)
4. **Frameworks & Templates** (downloadable resources)
5. **Reflection Questions** (2-3 per lesson)
6. **Exercises** (practical application)
7. **Further Reading** (books and resources)
