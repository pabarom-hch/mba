# Module 21: Leadership

## Phase 8: Leadership & Organization | Duration: 3 Weeks

---

## Module Overview

Leadership is the art and science of influencing others to achieve shared goals. This module explores leadership theories, emotional intelligence, and the practical skills needed to lead effectively in complex organizational environments. You will develop your personal leadership philosophy while mastering frameworks for leading change and inspiring teams.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon completing this module, you will be able to:

1. **Analyze** major leadership theories and their applications
2. **Develop** emotional intelligence competencies for effective leadership
3. **Apply** different leadership styles to varying situations
4. **Design** change management initiatives using proven frameworks
5. **Craft** compelling visions and communicate them effectively
6. **Cultivate** executive presence and personal leadership brand

## Key Leadership Frameworks Summary

| Framework | Developer | Key Insight |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Trait Theory | Various | Leaders possess inherent characteristics |
| Behavioral Theory | Ohio State/Michigan | Leadership is learnable behavior |
| Situational Leadership | Hersey & Blanchard | Adapt style to follower readiness |
| Transformational | Burns/Bass | Inspire through vision and values |
| Servant Leadership | Greenleaf | Lead by serving others |
| Authentic Leadership | George | Lead from core values |
| Level 5 Leadership | Collins | Humility + professional will |
| Adaptive Leadership | Heifetz | Mobilize for tough challenges |
