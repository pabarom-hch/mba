# Module 22: Organizational Behavior

## Phase 8: Leadership & Organization | Duration: 3 Weeks

---

## Module Overview

Organizational Behavior (OB) is the study of how people behave individually and in groups within organizations. This module provides frameworks for understanding motivation, team dynamics, organizational culture, power dynamics, and conflict management. Mastering these concepts enables leaders to create high-performing organizations where people thrive.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon completing this module, you will be able to:

1. **Explain** motivation theories and their application to employee engagement
2. **Analyze** group dynamics and stages of team development
3. **Build** high-performing teams using research-based models
4. **Assess** organizational culture and drive cultural change
5. **Navigate** organizational politics and power dynamics effectively
6. **Manage** conflict constructively using negotiation frameworks

## Key OB Concepts Preview

### Individual Level

| Concept | Definition | Business Impact |
|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Motivation | Internal drive to achieve goals | Productivity, engagement |
| Personality | Stable patterns of behavior | Job fit, team dynamics |
| Perception | How we interpret the world | Decision quality, bias |
| Attitudes | Evaluative statements about objects | Job satisfaction, commitment |

### Group Level

| Concept | Definition | Business Impact |
|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Team Cohesion | Degree of member attraction to group | Performance, retention |
| Psychological Safety | Belief that risk-taking is safe | Innovation, learning |
| Groupthink | Desire for conformity over quality | Decision quality |
| Social Loafing | Reduced effort in groups | Productivity |

### Organizational Level

| Concept | Definition | Business Impact |
|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Culture | Shared values, beliefs, assumptions | Strategy execution, talent |
| Structure | How work is divided and coordinated | Efficiency, agility |
| Climate | Perceived work environment | Engagement, satisfaction |
| Politics | Influence activities outside formal roles | Career success, change |
